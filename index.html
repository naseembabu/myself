Hi, I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. in the Computer Science & Engineering Department at the Indian Institute of Technology Patna, India. I am working under the supervision of Professor Jimson Mathew (IIT Patna) and Professor Vinod A Prasad (Singapore Institute of Technology, Singapore). My research areas include biomedical signal processing, audio signal processing, brain-computer interface, machine learning, and deep learning.

I received my M.Tech degree in Computer Engineering from Malviya National Institute of Technology Jaipur, Rajasthan, India, in 2020, and my B.Tech degree in Information Technology from Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam Technical University Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh, India, in 2017.
